Course Description & Info

Welcome to "Introduction to Machine Learning 419(M)". This is a UG Minor course. You would be introduced about basic machine learning methods in this course. In particular, we will specifically focus on foundations on machine learning from somewhat formal viewpoint (but not too formal) and then will discuss how to apply those concepts to solve a real world problem.

There are no official prerequisites. However, those who have solid foundations on probability and linear algebra will find the content very easy to follow
