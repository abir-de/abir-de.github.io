<p id=about vspace="-35"> </p> 
      <rintx>About me</rintx>
<p vspace="35"> </p> 
<p  align="justify">
I am an Assistant Professor at the <a href="https://www.cse.iitb.ac.in/">Department of Computer Science and Engineering</a> in  <a href="https://www.iitb.ac.in/">IIT Bombay</a> since January 2020. 
Before that, I did my postdoctoral research with <a href="https://people.mpi-sws.org/~manuelgr/">Manuel Gomez Rodriguez</a> in <a href="https://www.mpi-sws.org/">Max Planck Institute for Software Systems at Kaiserslautern, Germany</a>. 
I received my PhD from <a href="http://cse.iitkgp.ac.in/">Department of Computer Science and Engineering</a>, <a href="http://www.iitkgp.ac.in/">IIT Kharagpur</a> where I was advised by <a href="http://www.facweb.iitkgp.ernet.in/~niloy/">Prof. Niloy Ganguly</a>.
During that time, I was a part of the <a href="http://cse.iitkgp.ac.in/resgrp/cnerg/">Complex Network Research Group (CNeRG)</a> at IIT Kharagpur. My PhD work was supported by <a href="https://india.googleblog.com/2013/06/the-2013-google-india-phd-fellowships.html"> Google India PhD Fellowship 2013</a>.
<br> 
Prior to this I did my BTech in Electrical Engineering  and MTech in Control Systems Engineering both from <a href="www.iitkgp.ac.in">IIT Kharagpur</a>. 
</p>

<p id=res vspace="-35"> </p> 
      <rintx>Research Interests</rintx>
<p vspace="35" align="justify"> </p> 
<p  align="justify">
 My broad area of research interest is machine learning. My current research focus is designing machine learning models
 and methods for structured objects namely graphs and sets. Some examples of the current problems I am working are:
 <li><descry> Designing graph and set embeddings which can bypass the side-effects of traditional symmetric aggregators </descry> </li>
  <li><descry> Developing Neural networks which can learn combinatorial optimization algorithms on graphs and sets </descry> </li>
  <li><descry> Data subset selection with applications to efficient learning, active learning, human assisted learning </descry> </li>
 <li><descry> Human centric learning on graphs, e.g., designing privacy preserving algorithms, strategic learning, etc. </descry> </li>
  </p>
